Welcome to VØID 1.2
VØID 1.2 is an advanced, fully offline audio slicer and remixer. It runs entirely in your browser without any cloud processing.

It was built to help you rapidly break down stems, isolate precise hits, and recombine them into something entirely new.
Drop, Slice, Discover
How it works: Drop up to 10 audio files into the dropzone.

Choose your exact slice granularity—from full 8-bar phrases, 1/16th chops, or specific transients to silence-separated bits.

Hit "Slice all songs" and watch VØID instantly build your sample library locally.
Build the Remainder
The End Product: Play your sliced stems live in the Pad Player (switch tabs above). Map to keys, record performances, and export Ableton Drum Racks directly.

Or, use the Local Remixer to auto-generate a new arrangement based on energy, structure, or randomness.
