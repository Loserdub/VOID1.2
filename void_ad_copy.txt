# VØID 1.2 — Offline Audio Slicer & Remixer

## Highlight Features (For Video Reel Captions)
- **Zero-Latency Offline Slicing**: Instantly slice entire folders of audio—no cloud needed.
- **Granular Slice Modes**: Cut by phrase, triplet, downbeat, or transient peaks.
- **Algorithmic Local Remixer**: Instantly auto-remix your slices using Euclidean rhythms, Markov chains, or Golden Ratio builds.
- **Playable Pad Bank**: Drag and drop slices to a 16-pad sampler. Play your keyboard. Perform.
- **Sample Refinement Engine**: Seamlessly trim, rename, and micro-edit any slice directly in the app.
- **Ableton Drum Rack Export**: Turn your custom pad bank into a ready-to-use Ableton kit with one click.
